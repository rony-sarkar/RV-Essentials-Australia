RV Electronics Water Tank Gauge Single Tank Black LED Level Indicator Real Time


Never worry about running dry again with the RV Electronics 12V Water Tank Gauge. This single tank LED water level indicator connects to a fixed sensor probe inside your water tank, providing real-time information about your water levels. Say goodbye to guessing when it's time to refill, thanks to this user-friendly, DIY-friendly gauge.

Enhance the functionality of your caravan's cupboard doors with our Set of 5 Black Cupboard Door and Drawer Lock Strikers. Crafted in Italy for exceptional quality, these versatile strikers are designed to fit locks used on popular caravan brands such as Jayco, JB, Hilltop, Spreme, New Age, and more.

  • 1. Real-Time Display:

    Keep a constant eye on your water levels with the real-time display, ensuring you're always aware of how much water you have left.

    This gauge is suitable for both plastic and metal tanks, making it a versatile addition to your motorhome or caravan.

  • 3. Easy DIY Installation:

    No need to hire a professional. Easily wire this gauge yourself, with a variety of wiring options to suit your specific vehicle.

    Customize your kit to meet your exact needs with a range of single tank, double tank, and triple tank gauges. Pair them with 2.5-meter, 5-meter, or 7.5-meter lead sender probes.
